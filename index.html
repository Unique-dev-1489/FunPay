Terms & Conditions

1. Educational Purpose Only

Fun Pay is an educational platform designed to teach users about digital payment systems. All transactions are simulated and no real money is involved.

2. Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials, including their PIN. Sharing account details is strictly prohibited.

3. Platform Usage

The platform should be used for educational purposes only. Any misuse, including attempts to manipulate the system for personal gain, may result in account suspension.

4. Data Privacy

User data is collected for educational purposes and is not shared with third parties. The platform does not store any financial information as no real transactions occur.

5. Platform Availability

Fun Pay is provided "as is" for educational purposes. The service may be temporarily unavailable for maintenance or updates without prior notice.

6. Age Restriction

Users under the age of 13 should not use this platform without parental supervision. The platform is designed primarily for educational use by teenagers and young adults.

By using Fun Pay, you acknowledge that this is an educational platform and not a real financial service.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my PIN?

Go to Profile > Change PIN. You'll need to enter your current PIN to set a new one.

Is my money real?

No, all money in Fun Pay is virtual and for educational purposes only.

How do I find my account number?

Your account number is displayed on your virtual debit card in the Profile section.

Can I add real money?

No, real money cannot be added. Only administrators can adjust balances for educational purposes.
